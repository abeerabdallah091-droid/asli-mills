About Us

Who We Are

Asli Mills was founded on 5th February 1998. We have become a leading producer for reliable and natural products by producing 100% natural products with quality, reasonably priced, and environmentally friendly.

From the ancient Frankincense forests of the Horn of Africa to the time-honoured Qasil leaf trusted by generations of Somali women — every product we offer carries centuries of natural wisdom.
